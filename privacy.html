Privacy Policy

This document describes the data collected and processed by the "RemoteFolder" app. By using the app, users agree to the privacy policy. If you do not agree with any part of this privacy policy, it is recommended not to use our services.

1. Information Collection and Use

All information created by the user is stored in the app's internal storage. All data downloaded by the user is stored in the location specified on the phone at the time of download. The company does not collect this information.

The company may collect non-personally identifiable information, such as device information and usage statistics, to improve user experience and maintain service quality.

AdMob

The company uses Google's AdMob service to display ads within the service. AdMob may use non-personal information, such as device information, to provide personalized ads that may interest you. For more details about AdMob's privacy practices, please visit the Privacy Policy.

Firebase Crashlytics

Firebase Crashlytics is used to identify and fix bugs within the service by collecting log data. This may include device information, app version, and other technical data. For more details about Google's privacy practices, please visit the Privacy Policy.

Firebase Analytics

Firebase Analytics is used to investigate and understand user interaction patterns with the service. This service collects information such as app usage statistics and device information. For more details, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

2. Information Sharing with Third Parties

The company may share non-personal information with third-party services for the following purposes:
